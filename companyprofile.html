Company Profile

The group (RMC) traditionally has been serving the construction sector as one of the leaders in the Nepal market for the last 3 decades. The trust of consumers and stakeholders in our group is to be continued through RMC Foods.

RMC Foods Pvt. Ltd. was established in the year 2021 with a focus on the manufacturing and processing of foods and a related range of products for Nepal and export markets.

Continuing the decades-old tradition, the group established state-of-the-art and one of the biggest oil processing factories in the industrial belt of Nepal (Jeetpur, Bara) with plans on the table for more products to be added simultaneously. In the pursuit of expansion and innovation, RMC Foods proudly introduces Nutriwell, a bakery Shortening product to revolutionize the key ingredient required for baking purpose, suitable for both professional or at home baking, ensuring exquisite taste and texture.

With the aim to establish itself in Nepalese and neighboring markets, the company collaborates with the biggest names in the marketing & branding sector, and sales channels are based on continuous and hassle-free market presence. The intention of all this is to bring quality and standard products to our consumers.

‘RMC’ as a group is already a trusted brand, and we are resolute in winning the hearts of every houses through RMC Foods, too.
